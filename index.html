About Me

OJT Trainee at DPWH!

Hello! I'm Rey John, a 3rd Year student currently undergoing my On-the-Job Training (OJT) at the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH). With a solid foundation in hardware repair and networking, I'm dedicated to ensuring the smooth operation of computer systems and networks. During my OJT, I've been actively involved in various projects that have enhanced my skills in diagnosing and repairing hardware issues, as well as setting up and maintaining network infrastructure. I'm excited to share my journey, projects, and the valuable lessons I've learned along the way. My goal is to ensure that all systems are running efficiently and effectively, providing a reliable technological environment. Let's connect and explore how we can build something amazing together!
